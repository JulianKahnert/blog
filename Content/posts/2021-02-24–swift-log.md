In this blogpost I want to talk about the logging framework from the Swift Server Work Group (SSWG).
It was [accepted in 2019](https://swift.org/server/) and is called [SwiftLog](https://github.com/apple/swift-log).

After my first view on this framework I was a little confused and did not get the main purpose and benefits of having a standardized logging API.
That's why I want to try my best here to sum it up ik my own words.

* [´Logger´](https://github.com/apple/swift-log/blob/12d3a8651d32295794a850307f77407f95b8c881/Sources/Logging/Logging.swift#L38)
